My Story

Since the age of 14, I have wanted nothing more than my own restaurant, and here I am 8 years later turning that dream into a reality. My goal is to bring you the best and most authentic Somali cuisine along with the best possible prices. There will be various flavours and dishes available to you, but also the option to customise and mix & match menu items is available to you as well.

I also have an avid passion for food blogging, so here you'll find loads of recipes and tips on here to help you master everything from Somali rice to delectable desserts. You can get into the kitchen yourself and find your own love for cooking!

I hope you enjoy eating with headovermeals.co!
